Tipsy

Tipsy is an annual spring dance competition that happens before spring break. Each team that sign up can contain up to 9 people. Solo dancers are competing against each other. This event is not only open to members of the organization but allows all students in The New School to join. Each team will need a leader in charge of all communication. Each team will need to fill and hand in an information sheet by an assigned date.
