Drop the Beat

Drop the Beat is an annual fall dance performance that happens before Thanksgiving break. All members of the club are required to sign-up and participate. According to the number of members in the organization, we will separate people into groups. Each group will then decide a leader who will be in charge of all the communication. Each team will need to fill and hand in an information sheet by an assigned date.


Drop the Beat Fall 2020 is cancelled due to Covid19.
