Flash Mob @ Washington Sq Park

D4nce Mob is a dance performance that will not be publicized. The members of the organization will prepare a series of dances to perform at a random time in Washington Square Park. This event could happen anytime during the school year depending on the school calendar. All members of the organization will need to participate. Details of each dance mob will be instructed in meetings by the leaders.
